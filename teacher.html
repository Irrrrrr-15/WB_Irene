How It Works
  • `teacher.html` is your editor.
  • `index.html` is the fixed student page.
  • `data/today.json` is the shared lesson file.
  • Students on other devices need a public video URL, not a local upload.

If you want students on other phones or computers to see the video, the video must already be online with a public URL, or you need a real storage/backend service.
